Education across the globe

As the world continues to become more technologically advanced, the importance of education is ever increasing. Education is key to improving society, as it is through education that people have been able to become more specialised and pass on their knowledge to future generations.

Therefore by looking at how educated different countries are, we can see how technologically advanced they are and how they compare to other neighbouring countries and the rest of the world.

Education & Quality of Life

Education is a key factor in determining the quality of life (QoL) for a given population of people. Therefore it is important to be able to measure the average levels of education across a region to be able to understand how well the people in the region are faring.

By comparing education levels to other QoL factors such as life expectancy and income, this relationship between education and QoL can be better understood.
